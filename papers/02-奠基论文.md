# 🏛️ 奠基论文 (Foundational Papers)

> 定义TTA领域的开创性工作，理解这些论文是深入研究TTA的基础。

## 论文列表

| # | 论文标题 | 作者 | 发表venue | 链接 | 核心贡献 |
|:-:|---------|------|-----------|------|---------|
| 1 | Test-Time Training with Self-Supervision for Generalization under Distribution Shifts (TTT) | Sun et al. | ICML 2020 | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1909.13231) | **开山之作**：提出通过自监督旋转预测辅助任务进行测试时训练 |
| 2 | Do We Really Need to Access the Source Data? Source Hypothesis Transfer (SHOT) | Liang et al. | ICML 2020 | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2002.08546) | 开创性无源域适应工作；冻结分类器，通过信息最大化学习目标特征提取器 |
| 3 | Improving Robustness against Common Corruptions by Covariate Shift Adaptation (AdaptiveBN) | Schneider et al. | NeurIPS 2020 | [GitHub](https://github.com/bethgelab/robustness) | 提出在测试时自适应调整批归一化统计量以减少协变量偏移 |
| 4 | Evaluating Prediction-Time Batch Normalization for Robustness (PredBN) | Nado et al. | ICML Workshop 2020 | — | 建议在预测时纯粹使用目标域BN统计量以增强鲁棒性 |
| 5 | Tent: Fully Test-Time Adaptation by Entropy Minimization | Wang et al. | ICLR 2021 | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.10726) | **里程碑论文**：通过最小化预测熵适应BN仿射参数，定义了完全测试时适应 |
| 6 | Adaptive Risk Minimization: Learning to Adapt to Domain Shift (ARM) | Zhang et al. | NeurIPS 2021 | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2007.02931) | 元学习框架，直接优化模型以实现有效的测试时适应能力 |

## 发展脉络

```
2020 ──── TTT (自监督辅助任务) ──── SHOT (无源域适应) ──── AdaptiveBN/PredBN (BN统计量适应)
  │
2021 ──── Tent (熵最小化，定义FTTA) ──── ARM (元学习框架)
  │
  ▼
  后续所有TTA方法的理论基础
```
